Bio

I care about the future of cities and its sustainable growth. I've a PhD in AI and Robotics and I've been a civic technology entrepreneur and software developer for the last +10 years. I'm the Co-Founder and CEO at OS City, a company leading AI and Blockchain implementations in the Latin-american public sector.

What it's interesting about me is that I've had the chance to live in the future, chatting with incredible humans like Bill Gates, Jack Ma, Peter Diamandis, Ray Kurzweil, David Roberts, Klaus Schwab... they made me an exponential thinker, they developed my massive transformative purpose, they showed me how to be a purpose-driven disruptor; and this is what I want to share with you...

I am a fellow at the highly selective Global Solutions Program at Singularity University 2017 focused on applying exponential technologies in finding impact solutions for global challenges at NASA Ames Research Center, Silicon Valley.

Areas of expertise

Hook & Scale

In this digital acceleration and abundant access to technology, any individual can build the new Facebook. But the question is who will use it? Creating new technological products is all about how we engage, how we innovate, how we address someone's goals, problems or needs; it's about answering the right questions to hook and scale.

Exponential Mindset

Breaking the linear mindset is essential to understand how different the future will be. It's said that the greatest shortcoming of the human race is our inability to understand exponential growth. We are used to gradual, incremental technological advancements, but the next 100 years of tech progress will seem like 20,000 years.

An Abundant Era

We're living our best numbers in literacy, life expectancy, democracies, and poverty reduction. Access to super computers is at everyone's hands, unicorns are no longer rare. Individuals are doing what companies used to do, and companies are doing what governments used to do. We shifted from a scarce to abundant.

Sustainability & Relevance

It is said that millennials are the first generation that will suffer the impacts of climate change and the last one that can actually do something about it. We need to leverage the resource revolution and become a magnet for impact investing creating profit plus purpose. We need to be prepared and relevant if we want to succeed.

Data-driven Mindset

I sell tacos I don't need AI. I sell shoes I don't need AI. I hear that all the time, "AI is a trend and not really a need for my business". Well good luck when that taco's place in front of yours starts serving before the customer asks. Good look when the shoe competitor forecasts accurately new models at the right moment and price tag.

Cloud Development

My crowd-based developments have been in media such as Forbes and The New York Times, winning several awards and grants. They all are built on top of the most powerful cloud, leverage diverse data-streams to improve decisions, assess public sentiment (NLP), mine satellital imagery (Vision), do location intelligence, and other AI strategies.

Planetary Society

We're all riding the same spaceship towards the same future, but not so many understand the possibilities. Global organizations are doing the job for us to find our purpose see it as Sustainable Development Goals by the UN or Planetary models and competitions such as the Xprize. We need to think at this scale a do our moonshot.

Dedicated Transformation

There over a 100 exponential technologies, every single one serving as a tool for disruption every single industry. The possibility to create a roadmap for a short, medium and long term transformation of your organization is at hand. Food industry, healthcare, finance, governance, communications, education, transportation...
